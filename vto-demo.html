Link your 3D avatar

HIGHCLASS uses Ready Player Me avatars for 3D virtual try‑on. Create or edit your avatar with the button on the right and we will capture the avatar URL here.

How this demo works

  1. Click “Open Ready Player Me”.
  2. Create or customize your avatar and click Done.
  3. Avatar GLB URL will appear below — used in future VTO scenes.
